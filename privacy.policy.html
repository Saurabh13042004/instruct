Welcome to Instruct

Instruct is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.
This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you access or use our educational platform, Instruct, which provides Quality learning resources via YouTube and other potential platforms.


By using our Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.


1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information from users:

1.1 Personal Information


Personal information is any information that can be used to identify you as an individual. This may include, but is not limited to:

    ● Name

    ● Email address

    ● Contact details (if provided)

    ● Any other personal details voluntarily shared with us, such as through contact forms or registration for newsletters.

1.2 Non-Personal Information


Non-personal information is data that cannot be used to identify you personally. This includes:

    ● Browser type and version

    ● Device type

    ● IP address

    ● Usage data (such as pages visited, time spent on our platform, etc.)

    ● Cookies and tracking technologies


2. How We Collect Information

We collect information in several ways:

    ● Directly from you: When you interact with us via contact forms, email inquiries, or voluntarily provide details for newsletters or feedback.

    ● Automatically: Through cookies and similar technologies, we collect non-personal information when you visit our website or access our content on YouTube.


3. Use of Information

We use the information collected to:

    ●Provide, maintain, and improve the functionality of our Platform.

    ●Respond to inquiries, feedback, and requests.

    ●Analyze user trends and behavior to enhance our educational content.

    ●Communicate with you, including responding to support requests or sending updates (such as newsletters, if subscribed).

    ●Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms and Conditions.


4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help us provide a better user experience. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

    ●Understand user preferences and improve site functionality.

    ●Analyze traffic and user interactions on our website.

    ●Offer personalized content based on user behavior.

You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to fully use our Platform.


5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to unauthorized third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

    With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers for business purposes, such as assisting in website operations, conducting business activities, or servicing you. In all such cases, we will enter into a formal agreement with these service providers to ensure the non-misuse of user data. If any party breaches this agreement, they will be held fully liable for any misuse or violation of the agreement.

    For Legal Purposes: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal process.

    Business Transfers: In the unlikely event that

    Instruct is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your personal data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.


6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. However, no transmission of data over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information transmitted through our Platform. Once we receive your information, we implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.


7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding the personal information we hold about you:

    Access: You can request access to your personal information.

    Correction: You can request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

    Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information under certain circumstances.

    Opt-out: You can opt-out of receiving newsletters or promotional emails from us at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in these communications.


8. Links to Other Websites

Our Platform may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by

Instruct. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party websites before engaging with their services.


9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to this policy will be reflected on this page, along with the updated date. Your continued use of our Platform after changes to the policy indicates your acceptance of the revised terms.
